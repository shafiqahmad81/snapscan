How do I download SnapScan?

Open the SnapScan app and tap on "Scan" to scan the SnapCode (a QR code, or square barcode) displayed in the shop, at the point of sale, on the invoice or website where you want to pay. This identifies the shop and prompts you to enter the amount you wish to pay.
